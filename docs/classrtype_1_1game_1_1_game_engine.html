◆ handleHealthPackSpawns()

void rtype::game::GameEngine::handleHealthPackSpawns ( )
private

Handles the spawning of health packs.

Handles the spawning of health packs in the game world.

This function is responsible for periodically spawning health packs at random positions within the game area. The logic ensures that health packs do not spawn on top of walls and others health packs.

  • Spawning frequency is controlled by a timer, with a delay of 10.2 seconds between spawns and 4 max has spawn in map.
  • Health packs are assigned random x and y coordinates within the boundaries of the game area:
    • x: [0, 800]
    • y: [0, 600]
  • A check is performed to ensure that health packs do not overlap with any wall entity by using the checkCollisionRect function.
  • Once a valid position is found, a new entity is created with the following components:
    • Position: The randomly generated position.
    • HealthBonus: A bonus value of 3.
    • Velocity: Set to zero as health packs do not move.
Note
The function ensures that health packs are spawned in valid locations and with appropriate attributes.
